Story 1: A Hero for Education
Malala Yousafzai is a young woman from Pakistan who is known as a hero for her work in education.
馬拉拉·優薩福扎伊是一位來自巴基斯坦的年輕女性，以她在教育方面的工作而被譽為英雄。
She believes strongly in the power of learning and wants every child, especially girls, to go to school.
她堅信學習的力量，並希望每個孩子，特別是女孩，都能接受教育。
When she was a child, some people in her town said that girls should not go to school.
當她還是個孩子時，鎮上的一些人認為女孩不應該上學。
But Malala and her family did not agree.
但馬拉拉和她的家人不同意。
She spoke up for education and wanted to make a difference in her community.
她為教育發聲，並希望為社區帶來改變。
When Malala was just 15 years old, she was hurt very badly by people who did not want her to speak.
當馬拉拉僅15歲時，一些不希望她發聲的人對她造成了嚴重傷害。
But she was strong. She recovered and continued her fight for Quality Education and Gender Equality.
但她很堅強，康復後繼續為優質教育和性別平等而奮鬥。
She believes that every girl should have the chance to learn and grow.
她相信每個女孩都應該有學習和成長的機會。
In 2014, Malala received the Nobel Peace Prize.
2014年，馬拉拉獲得了諾貝爾和平獎。
She was only 17, the youngest person to ever win this prize.
她當時只有17歲，是獲得此獎項最年輕的人。
She was honored because of her courage and her work for Quality Education for all children.
她因為勇氣和為所有孩子爭取優質教育的工作而受到表彰。
Malala's work is also connected to the Sustainable Development Goals by the world, and goal number four is about Quality Education.
馬拉拉的工作也與全球的可持續發展目標相連，其中第四個目標是關於優質教育。
Malala is a role model for many people, and her story shows us that one person can make a big difference.
馬拉拉是許多人的榜樣，她的故事告訴我們一個人也能帶來巨大改變。
Malala continues to inspire people with her story and her efforts to bring education to all children.
馬拉拉持續以她的故事和努力啟發人們，讓所有孩子都能接受教育。
She shows that with courage and determination, we can work towards a better world with Gender Equality and opportunities for everyone to learn and grow.
她展示了憑藉勇氣和決心，我們可以邁向一個性別平等且每個人都有學習和成長機會的更美好世界。
Malala Yousafzai is truly a hero of our time.
馬拉拉·優薩福扎伊真的是我們這個時代的英雄。
Story 2: Respecting Different Cultures
One day at school, Isaka and his friend Thomas were sitting in the lunchroom.
一天在學校，Isaka和他的朋友Thomas正在午餐室裡坐著。
Isaka had brought some delicious food from home, and it looked different from the sandwiches Thomas usually ate.
Isaka從家裡帶來了一些美味的食物，與Thomas平常吃的三明治不同。
"What's that, Isaka?" Thomas asked, curious.
Thomas好奇地問道：「那是什麼，Isaka？」
Isaka smiled and said, "This is called sushi. It's a traditional food from Japan."
Isaka微笑著說：「這叫做壽司，是日本的傳統食品。」
My family eats it for special occasions.
我的家人在特別的場合會吃它。
Thomas's eyes widened. "Wow, it looks so interesting! What's inside?"
Thomas眼睛睜大了：「哇，看起來真有趣！裡面有什麼？」
Isaka explained, "It's made with rice, fish, and sometimes vegetables, all wrapped in seaweed. My mom taught me how to make it. Would you like to try some?"
Isaka解釋說：「這是用米飯、魚，有時候還有蔬菜，全部用海藻包裹而成。我媽媽教我怎麼做。你想嘗嘗嗎？」
Thomas hesitated for a moment. "I've never had sushi before. I'm not sure if I'll like it."
Thomas猶豫了一會兒：「我從來沒吃過壽司，我不確定我是否會喜歡它。」
Isaka said, "That's okay! It's great to try new things, even if they're different. You might like it!"
Isaka說：「沒關係！嘗試新事物總是好的，即使它們不同，你可能會喜歡的！」
Thomas smiled and agreed, "Okay, I'll give it a try!"
Thomas微笑著同意道：「好吧，我試試看！」
After taking a bite, Thomas's face lit up. "It's really good! It's so fresh and different from what I'm used to, but I like it!"
咬了一口之後，Thomas的臉上綻放出笑容：「真的很好吃！它既新鮮又與我習慣的不同，但我喜歡！」
Isaka laughed. "I'm glad! In my culture, food is a way to show love and respect for others. It's fun to share our traditions."
Isaka笑了：「我很高興！在我的文化中，食物是表達對他人愛與尊重的一種方式。分享我們的傳統很有趣。」
Thomas thought for a moment. "I think it's cool that we all have different foods and traditions. We can learn a lot from each other."
Thomas想了一會兒：「我覺得我們大家都有不同的食物和傳統，這真酷，我們可以互相學習很多東西。」
Isaka nodded. "Exactly! That's what makes the world so interesting. We should always respect and appreciate each other's cultures."
Isaka點點頭說：「沒錯！這正是讓世界變得如此有趣的原因。我們應該始終尊重並欣賞彼此的文化。」
